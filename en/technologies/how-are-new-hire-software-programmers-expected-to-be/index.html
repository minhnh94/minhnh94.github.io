Doing great work on your first day will only raise the premature expectation on you, keep you busy to live up to it, and end up making you burnt out later. Therefore, knowing and adjusting yourself to be suitable for a long run would be a wiser and smarter choice. Lower your standard, and heighten it later.

2. To learn, you have to unlearn

Guess why companies prefer freshers to mid-careers? Freshers donâ€™t need time to unlearn what they were stuffed in the previous workplace!
